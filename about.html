ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cafe was established in a quaint little town by the visionary Ms. Anoc Situated amidst serene surroundings, it quickly became a beloved spot for locals and travelers alike to unwind and savor delicious delicacies. With a menu crafted with care and attention to detail, each dish tells a story of culinary passion and creativity. From the first sip of freshly brewed coffee to the last bite of decadent dessert, patrons are enveloped in an atmosphere of warmth and hospitality. Ms. Anoc dedication to excellence shines through in every aspect of The Cafe, making it a cherished destination for anyone seeking a memorable dining experience.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Kimjie Anoc

Opening hours: Open from 11am to 9pm
